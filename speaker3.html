MIYAZAKI Toshiaki

Department of Computer Science and Engineering /

Division of Computer Engineering

Biography

1981.3 Graduated from the University of Electro-Communications, Department of Applied Electronic Engineering. 1983.3 Completed the master's course at the same university. Doctor of Engineering (Tokyo Institute of Technology, 1994.9). 1983.4 Joined Nippon Telegraph and Telephone Public Corporation (now NTT). He is engaged in research and development of LSI-CAD, communication FPGA (Field Programmable Gate Array) and application equipment, active networks, and ubiquitous networks. Since April 2005, he has been a professor at the University of Aizu's Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering. Director of the University of Aizu, Dean of Computer Science and Engineering (2014.4-2020.3). Dean, Department of Computer and Information Systems, Graduate School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Aizu (2008.4-2014.3). Deputy Director, Advanced Information Science Research Center, University of Aizu (2012.4-2014.3). Director of the Center (2013.4-2014.3). Deputy Director of Reconstruction Support Center, University of Aizu (2013.4-2014.3). Part-time lecturer at Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology (2003-2007). Visiting Professor, Graduate School of Niigata University (2004). IEICE Advanced Open Lecturer (1996-2008).
